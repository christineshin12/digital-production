Olivia Dean wins best new artist at the 2026 Grammy Awards

By Christine Shin

Image source: Myung J. Chun / Los Angeles Times

The British pop singer-songwriter won her very first Grammy at Sunday's ceremony. She rose to fame last year through her album "The Art of Loving."

She took a moment to thank her team and then paid tribute to immigrants.

"I'm up here as a granddaughter of an immigrant," Dean said onstage. "I'm a product of bravery, and I think those people deserve to be celebrated. We're nothing without each other."
